Lyric analysis of 50 years of Billboard Top 100 songs

We looked at every song on the year-end Billboard Top 100 chart from 1965 to 2017 and analyzed the lyrics to find out how simple (percentage of words that are the 500 most common in the English language), repetitive (percentage of repeated words in a single song), and similar (percentage of shared words between two songs) they are.

You can move the slider to select top 100 songs from a year, or search for a word to see where it appears in the year's top song lyrics.
